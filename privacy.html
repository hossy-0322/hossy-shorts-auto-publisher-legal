Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Overview

Hossy Shorts Auto Publisher is a local-first creator tool. This policy explains how the app handles data when users prepare short videos and optionally connect TikTok to send inbox drafts.

2. Data Processed Locally

The app may process video files, captions, hashtags, generated metadata, platform state, and job history on the user's local machine. This local data is not sold and is not shared with third parties by default.

3. TikTok OAuth Data

When a user connects TikTok, the app uses TikTok OAuth to request permissions such as basic account access and video draft upload access. OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user's machine and are used only to perform actions requested by the user, such as sending a video draft to the user's TikTok inbox.

4. Data Shared With TikTok

The app sends data to TikTok only when the user explicitly starts a TikTok inbox upload. In that case, the selected video file and required upload metadata are sent to TikTok through TikTok's Content Posting API. The app does not automatically publish public TikTok posts.

5. No Sale of Personal Data

The app does not sell personal data. The app does not use TikTok OAuth tokens for advertising, profiling, or unrelated third-party sharing.

6. Retention and Deletion

Local project files, metadata, and tokens remain on the user's machine until the user deletes them, removes the app data, or revokes access. Users can revoke TikTok access through TikTok's account or app authorization settings.

7. Security

The app is designed to store OAuth tokens locally and avoid displaying secrets in logs. Users should protect their device, local app data, and backups to prevent unauthorized access.

8. Third-Party Services

TikTok and other third-party platforms process data according to their own policies. Users should review the privacy policies and terms of any third-party services they connect to or use with this app.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the maintainer through the GitHub repository where this legal page is published.
