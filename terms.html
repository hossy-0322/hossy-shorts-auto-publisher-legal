1. Overview

Hossy Shorts Auto Publisher is a local-first creator workflow tool for preparing short videos, captions, and platform metadata. The app can use TikTok authorization to send video drafts to the authenticated user's TikTok inbox through TikTok's Content Posting API.

2. Manual Review and Posting

The app does not automatically publish public TikTok posts. TikTok inbox uploads are drafts. After a draft is sent, the user must open TikTok, review the video and caption, make any desired edits, and complete the post manually inside TikTok.

3. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the videos, captions, hashtags, music, rights, disclosures, and other content they prepare, upload, or publish. Users must comply with applicable laws, TikTok's terms, TikTok's community guidelines, and any platform rules that apply to their account.

4. TikTok Authorization

The app requests TikTok permissions only when the user chooses to connect their TikTok account. The requested permissions are used to identify the authorized account and send video drafts to that account's TikTok inbox. Users may revoke access through their TikTok account settings.

5. Local Storage

The app is designed to run locally. Project files, generated metadata, and OAuth tokens are stored on the user's machine unless the user explicitly sends a draft to TikTok. Users are responsible for protecting access to their own device and local files.
